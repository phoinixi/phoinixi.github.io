Hi there! I'm Francesco, a Senior Software Engineer. This is my personal space where I share what I learn and build to help others in their coding journey.

## My Background

With two decades of experience in Software Development, I've worked across various technologies and industries. I specialize in frontend development with a focus on React, TypeScript, and modern web tooling.

## What I'm Working On

- [Resuml](https://github.com/phoinixi/resuml): a CLI tool to manage your resume as YAML code, with validation and multi-format export
- [JSON Resume Theme: React](https://github.com/phoinixi/jsonresume-theme-react): a modern React 18 theme for JSON Resume with multi-language support and dark mode
- [Developer Dotfiles](https://github.com/phoinixi/dotfiles): my macOS frontend dev environment, automated with Homebrew and Oh-My-Zsh
